Player Profile & Percentile Rankings
Search for any NCAA player to see their season stats and percentile rankings. A percentile of 85 means the player ranks better than 85% of peers at the same position group and tier.

Position & Tier: Each player is ranked only against players in the same position group (Guard, Wing, Forward, Big) and the same tier (e.g. Power 5, High-Major, Mid-Major). This ensures fair comparisons.

Qualification: Players need ≥ 10 games played and ≥ 12 minutes per game to receive percentile rankings.

Radar chart: Each axis = percentile rank (0–100). Outer ring = 100th percentile. Gold ring = 50th percentile (average).

Season: Currently showing 2025–26 data.

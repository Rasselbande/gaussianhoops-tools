What is this tool?
The NCAA Similar Players finder identifies the most statistically comparable players in college basketball. Enter any player from the 2025-26 season, use the filters to narrow your search, and instantly find their 10 closest matches across 3,190 NCAA players. Built for coaches and scouts who want to ground player comparisons in data.

How the algorithm works

Each player is represented as a multi-dimensional vector of normalized statistics — scoring, playmaking, rebounding, defense, shooting efficiency, and overall impact. Normalization ensures that high-volume players don't automatically dominate the results.

Cosine similarity measures the angle between two player vectors: a score of 100% means identical statistical profiles, while lower scores indicate greater differences. The result is a ranked list of the 10 players whose overall statistical fingerprint most closely matches the reference player, filtered by your selected criteria.

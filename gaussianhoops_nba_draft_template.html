🏀 NBA DRAFT BOARD 2026
Prospect rankings powered by DCS v2 — Draft Ceiling Score, our research-validated draft metric built from five components: Physical Upside (PUI), Shooting Talent (STI), Athleticism (AC), Playmaking (PMI), and Scoring Efficiency (SEU). Age and class-year multipliers reward youth and penalise senior production. Competition-level adjustments discount Low-Major stats. Validated against 2020–2024 draft classes: ρ = 0.39 Spearman correlation with actual pick numbers (vs ρ = 0.10 for raw stats).
Validation: DCS v2 tested on 5 draft classes (2020–2024, n≈230 picks). Best in 2023 (ρ = +0.55). Measurements data for current prospects improves as combine season approaches. This is a ceiling model — it intentionally overweights youth and physical tools.

DCS v2 is a composite draft-ceiling signal. Unlike production metrics (PPG, PER), it weights attributes that translate to the NBA over college dominance that doesn't.

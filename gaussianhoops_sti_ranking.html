Shooting Talent Index — Shooting Ability Ranking
The STI measures a player's overall shooting talent on a 0–100 percentile scale, ranked across the full NCAA pool. Higher = better shooter relative to all qualified college basketball players this season. Click any column header to re-sort. Toggle Portal only to filter transfer portal players.

STI is a composite percentile score combining four shooting metrics, each Bayesian-adjusted for sample size:

ComponentWeightDescription
3P% (Bayesian adj.)38%3-point percentage corrected for attempts volume
FT% (Bayesian adj.)27%Free throw percentage corrected for attempts volume
TS%25%True Shooting % — overall scoring efficiency
3PAr (normalized)10%3-point attempt rate — rewards players who shoot from range

The composite is then percentile-ranked across all 2285 qualified NCAA players. A score of 90+ = top 10% shooter in college basketball.
